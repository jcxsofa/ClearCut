📋 How to use in Cricut Design Space
  1. Open Cricut Design Space on your computer or tablet.
  2. Click Upload in the left panel.
  3. Click Upload Image, then select the downloaded .svg file.
  4. Choose Cut Image and click Save.
  5. Insert the image into your project and size it to match your sheet if needed.
  6. Place your laminated sheet on the Cricut mat aligned to the top-left corner.
  7. Load the mat into your Cricut and click Make It → Cut!

ClearCut — all processing happens on your device. No data is ever uploaded.
